# Strategic Narrative: Nexus CareSight (The "Why")

## Executive Value Proposition
As a BI Analyst, my goal is to drive functional efficiency and focus on standardization. **Nexus CareSight** was built to demonstrate how unstructured clinical data—often trapped in provider shift notes—can be standardized into a structured, actionable SQL-backed risk engine.

## Mapping to Core BI Analytics Requirements

### 1. Focus on Standardization & Efficiency
*   **Need**: Enhancing work efficiency through standardization of technical tools.
*   **Solution**: Implemented a **Clinical Logic Engine** that standardizes messy, non-standard medical shorthand (e.g., "Pt A/O x1") into discrete, queryable data fields. This allows an ACO to perform population-level analytics that were previously trapped in unstructured text.

### 2. Clinical Intelligence vs. Paraphrasing
*   **Value**: Demonstrating advanced synthesis over simple summarization.
*   **Logic**: The system doesn't just "read" the note; it performs **Clinical Risk Stratification** by flagging Evidence-Based markers (Cognitive, Metabolic, Physical) and calculating a compounding risk delta based on historical context.

### 3. Technical Risk Mitigation
*   **Need**: Identify and analyze project and technical risks & impacts.
*   **Solution**: Addressed "Alert Fatigue" by implementing compounding risk-score thresholds (30/70) that only escalate when multiple decline markers (cognitive + mobility) intersect over a 48-hour window.

## Industry Alignment (SNF & Value-Based Care)
This project focuses on **Reducing Avoidable Hospitalizations**—the core metric for Value-Based Care (VBC) models and Special Needs Plans (SNP). By surfacing clinical flags 48 hours earlier, this tool demonstrates a direct path to improved financial outcomes and patient dignity.
